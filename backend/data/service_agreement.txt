SERVICE AGREEMENT

1. Scope of Services
The Service Provider agrees to provide IT consulting services as described in Annexure A.

2. Payment Terms
The Client shall pay INR 1,00,000 per month within 15 days of invoice submission.

3. Late Payment
Late payments shall incur interest at 2% per month.

4. Term and Renewal
This Agreement shall be valid for one year and shall automatically renew unless terminated with 60 days notice.

5. Termination
Either party may terminate for material breach if not cured within 30 days.

6. Limitation of Liability
The Service Provider’s liability shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement.

7. Indemnity
The Client agrees to indemnify the Service Provider against third-party claims arising from misuse of services.

8. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of sensitive information.

9. Data Protection
Both parties shall comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR where applicable.

10. Dispute Resolution
Disputes shall be resolved through arbitration under Indian Arbitration Act.
