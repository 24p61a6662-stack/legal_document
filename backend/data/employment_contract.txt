EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

1. Position and Duties
The Employee shall serve as Software Developer and perform duties assigned by the Employer. The Employee agrees to devote full working time and attention to the business.

2. Compensation
The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of INR 50,000 payable at the end of each month. Bonuses may be awarded at the Employer's discretion.

3. Confidentiality
The Employee agrees not to disclose confidential business information, trade secrets, or client data during or after employment.

4. Term and Termination
This Agreement shall commence on the joining date and continue until terminated. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

5. Intellectual Property
All work products created during employment shall be the exclusive property of the Employer.

6. Non-Compete
The Employee shall not engage in competing business activities during employment and for six months after termination.

7. Data Protection
The Employee shall comply with all applicable data protection and privacy laws including GDPR where applicable.

8. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India under the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

9. Liability
The Employee shall not be held liable for indirect damages unless caused by willful misconduct.

10. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in Hyderabad, India.
